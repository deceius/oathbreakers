FAQ

The meat and potatoes. Who we are, in detail.

Where is the Guild based?

Our primary content is on Northern BZ, but we have a Hideout in the Roads of Avalon. We do content wherever the Avalon Portals take us. Why stay on just one city?

Since The Oathbreakers is a PvP focused guild, how do you Fame Farm?

We have a Mentorship program from within the guild that has the best mentors that will help you to fame up, and if you're one of the best in that program, you are not going home empty-handed.

Taxes? Gathering?

We currently have 1% tax, which are mostly for HO food supply. Enjoy them silvers, yo. And for gathering, we are avid gatherers too. We have this so-called "Gathering CTA" where we just chill and get rich.

I'm not that strong, can I still join The Oathbreakers?

Yes, of course. Fame farm is the key, and to further help with that, we have guides on what to spec-up so you'll be an effective player in the game. Apes together stronk.
