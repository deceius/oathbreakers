FAQ

The meat and potatoes. Who we are, in detail.

Where is the Guild based?

Our BZ Outposts / Mage Raid content is on Thetford BZ, but we have a Hideout in the Roads of Avalon. We do content wherever the Avalon Portals take us. Why stay on just one city?

Since The Oathbreakers is a PvP focused guild, how do you Fame Farm?

We occassionally do Avalonian Dungeon runs for the Ascension Buff (Fame Bonus), and aside that, our Hideout in the Roads is in T7 zone with Green and Blue chest. That should suffice for fame farming (or you can just ask the members, they're usually game to do anything.)

Taxes? Gathering?

We currently have 0% taxes. Enjoy them silvers, yo. And for gathering, we are avid gatherers too. We have this so-called "Gathering CTA" where we just chill and get rich.

I'm not that strong, can I still join The Oathbreakers?

Yes, of course. Fame farm is the key, and to further help with that, we have guides on what to spec-up so you'll be an effective player in the game. Apes together stronk.
